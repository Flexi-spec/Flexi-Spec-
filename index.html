01. The Genesis

Born in November 2011, Ogunbiyi Jesutomisin emerged from a lineage of educators and clergy. His upbringing in Lagos, Nigeria, provided a backdrop of both vibrant energy and rigorous discipline.

"I didn't start with code; I started with curiosity. My father's torchlight was my first motherboard. I had to know why the light stayed on even when the room went dark."

02. Spiritual Focus

As a clergyman’s son, his perspective on technology is deeply philosophical. His seminal writing, "When Faith Meets the Feed," argues that digital algorithms shouldn't dictate human purpose.

03. Dismantling Logic

At age 12, Jesutomisin moved from mechanical disassembly to digital deconstruction. He mastered HTML and CSS not through books, but by inspecting the source code of the sites he visited, treating every webpage as a puzzle to be solved.
